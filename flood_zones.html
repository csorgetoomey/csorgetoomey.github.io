New Orleans Flood Zones

This map shows flood zones across three parishes in Louisiana, St Bernard, Orleans, and Jefferson. The focus of the map is the New Orleans area where evacuation spots are located. In case of an emergency these areas are used to make the evacuations smoother and more efficient.
